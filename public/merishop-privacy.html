Privacy Policy for MeriShop

Last updated: June 18, 2026

1. Introduction

ArovenTech operates the MeriShop mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information.

2. Information We Collect

MeriShop is an offline-first application. We do not transmit your business data to any external servers. The App requires Bluetooth (for printing), Camera (for barcode scanning), and Storage (for saving and sharing bills via WhatsApp) permissions.

3. Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal or business information. Your data never leaves your device unless you share an invoice using the built-in sharing features. It is your responsibility to secure your device to prevent unauthorized access to your local data.
